Thermostat Installation and Control Guide

The thermostat measures ambient temperature with an internal sensor and compares that reading against a stored target temperature.
When the measured temperature falls outside the configured threshold, the control unit sends commands to adjust HVAC heating or cooling output.
The thermostat supports Wi-Fi connectivity for mobile app control, remote monitoring, and software updates.
Technical setup instructions describe the thermostat's Wi-Fi provisioning workflow in detail.
