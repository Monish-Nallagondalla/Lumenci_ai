Lumenci Smart Thermostat Technical Overview

The thermostat contains an onboard ambient temperature sensor that samples room temperature every 30 seconds.
The controller compares sensed temperature values against the user-defined setpoint and issues heating or cooling commands to the HVAC system.
The device includes 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi for remote monitoring, OTA updates, and companion mobile application control.
Installation documentation explains how users pair the thermostat to the app through Wi-Fi provisioning.
The product specification notes that the thermostat automatically adjusts HVAC output when measured temperature deviates from the configured threshold.
