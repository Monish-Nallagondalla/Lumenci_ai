Lumenci Smart Thermostat
Product Specification and Installation Excerpts

Document: Technical Overview v2.4
Product: Lumenci Smart Thermostat

Section 2.1 - Temperature Sensing
The thermostat contains an internal ambient temperature sensor that samples room temperature every 30 seconds and forwards the measured value to the control board.

Section 2.3 - Control Logic
The controller compares the sensed room temperature against the user-configured setpoint and issues heating or cooling commands to the HVAC interface when the measured temperature falls outside the permitted threshold band.

Section 4.1 - Wireless Connectivity
The device includes a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi radio used for mobile application pairing, remote monitoring, and cloud-based settings synchronization.

Section 4.2 - Mobile App Provisioning
During onboarding, the installer connects the thermostat to the companion mobile application through Wi-Fi provisioning and account linking.
