NovaCam X2 Indoor Camera
Technical Notes and Setup Excerpts

Document: Hardware and Imaging Specification
Product: NovaCam X2 Indoor Camera

Section 1.2 - Imaging Hardware
The camera incorporates a CMOS image sensor that converts incoming light into digital image data for downstream image processing.

Section 3.1 - Wireless Connectivity
The device supports both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz Wi-Fi connections for remote live preview, mobile application control, and alert delivery.

Section 3.2 - App Onboarding
The setup guide instructs users to pair the camera with the companion application through Wi-Fi onboarding before enabling remote controls.

Section 5.4 - Exposure Control
Automatic exposure logic evaluates ambient lighting conditions and adjusts shutter speed and ISO settings in real time to maintain usable image brightness.
